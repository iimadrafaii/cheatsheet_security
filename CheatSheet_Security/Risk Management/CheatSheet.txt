Responses to Risk :
    Risk avoidance.
    Risk acceptance.
    Risk reduction.
    Risk sharing. ( buying an insurance )

Threat : Potential harm or danger to an individual, organisation, or system.
    Human-made threats
    Technical threats
    Natural threats

Vulnerability : A weakness in the system or software that can be exploited by a threat to cause harm.

Asset : An economic resource owned or controlled by an individual, company, or government.

Risk : The probability of a threat source exploiting an existing vulnerability (in an asset) 
        and resulting in adverse business effects.

Risk management : 
    Frame Risk
    Assess Risk
    Risk Analysis
    Respond to Risk
    Monitor Risk
